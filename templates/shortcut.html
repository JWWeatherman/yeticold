Installed Yeti on your Secondary Laptop?

Install Yeti on Secondary

Choose this option if you are installing Yeti for the first time or if you have erased your Secondary laptop since last running Yeti. If you have Ubuntu installed on your Secondary laptop you can check for Yeti by clicking the 9 dots in the bottom left corner and looking for the Yeti icons. If you don't see any Yeti icons you need to install Yeti on your Secondary laptop.

Skip Secondary Install

Choose this option if you already have Ubuntu and Yeti installed on your Secondary laptop. If you have Ubuntu installed on your Secondary laptop you can check for Yeti by clicking the 9 dots in the bottom left corner and looking for the Yeti icons. If you see any Yeti icons you should skip installing Yeti on your Secondary laptop.
