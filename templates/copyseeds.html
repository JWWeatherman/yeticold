Step {{step}}: Copy Seeds to CDs

  1. Insert your CD labeled "Seed1".
  2. Open the Files application and click on Documents.
  3. Copy the file named "yetiseed1.txt" to the CD and click Write to Disc and then click Burn.
  4. When you see "Data CD successfully burnt" click Close and remove the CD.
  5. Repeat steps 1 through 4 above with your other six seeds and seed CDs respectively then click Next to continue.

Ensure that you have a different seed on each of your seven seed CDs labeled "Seed 1" through "Seed 7" before clicking Next.
