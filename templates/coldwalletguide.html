Cold Wallet Guide

These steps will walk you through Sending and Reciving.

If you are following the testing flow Recieve and Send a small test amount and then go back to the other laptop.

Receive: Step 1

In the bitcoin core application on your Online Laptop click the Receive button in the menu bar.

Receive: Step 2

Click the "Create new receiving address" button outlined in red.

Receive: Finished

Confirm that your wallet (outlined in red) shows "yetiwalletpriv"(offline laptop) or "yetiwalletpub"(online laptop). You can now send money to the wallet using the QR code or the address displayed bellow it.

Send: Step 1

In the bitcoin core application on your Online Laptop click on the Send button (outlined in red).

Send: Step 2

Fill out this page including at least a recipent and an amount. Then click on "Create Unsigned" (outlined in red).

Send: Step 3

Review your transaction then click "Create Unsigned" (outlined in red).

Send: Step 4

Click the "Save" button (outlined in red).

Send: Step 5

Select the "Documents" Folder (outlined in red) and then click "Open" (outlined in orange).

Send: Step 6

Rename the file name(outlined in red) to "unsigned.psbt" then click "Save"(outlined in orange).

Send: Step 7

  1. Insert the usb drive labled "Transfer" to laptop.
  2. Delete all visable files in the USB drive.
  3. Copy the file labled unsigned.psbt to the inserted USB drive. The file is located in your Documents folder.
  4. Eject the USB drive.

Send: Step 8

  1. Switch to your Offline laptop
  2. Insert the usb drive labled "Transfer" to laptop.
  3. Delete all visable files in the USB drive.
  4. Copy the file labled unsigned.psbt to the "Documents" Folder. The file is located in your usb drive.
  5. Eject the USB drive.

Send: Step 9

In the bitcoin core application in the menu bar click file(outlined in red) > "Load PSBT from file..."

Send: Step 10

Select the "unsigned.psbt" file (outlined in red) and then click "Open"(outlined in orange).

Send: Step 11

Click on "Sign tx"(outlined in red).

Send: Step 12

Click on "Save" (outlined in red).

Send: Step 13

Select the "Documents" folder (outlined in red), Then rename the file to "signed.psbt" (outlined in orange), Then click "Save" (outlined in yellow).

Send: Step 14

  1. Insert the usb drive labled "Transfer" to laptop.
  2. Delete all visable files in the USB drive.
  3. Copy the file labled signed.psbt to the inserted USB drive. The file is located in your Documents folder.
  4. Eject the USB drive.

Send: Step 15

  1. Switch to your Online laptop
  2. Insert the usb drive labled "Transfer" to laptop.
  3. Delete all visable files in the USB drive.
  4. Copy the file labled signed.psbt to the "Documents" Folder. The file is located in your usb drive.
  5. Eject the USB drive.

Send: Step 16

In the bitcoin core application in the menu bar click file(outlined in red) > "Load PSBT from file..."

Send: Step 17

Select the "signed.psbt" file (outlined in red) located in your "Documents folder", Then click "Open" (outlined in orange).

Send: Step 18

Click on the "Broadcast Tx" button (outlined in red).

Send: Finished

You should see this page to confirm the transaction was broadcasted.
